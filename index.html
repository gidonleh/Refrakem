About Us

Refrakem Refractories & Chemicals Ltd. was established in 1971 by Maurice Kleeblatt. The basis of the company was exporting nozzles, produced in Israel, for the steel industry. The activities increased rapidly by the addition of Israeli chamotte exports and the export of refractory bricks produced by Hasin Esh. The export of the Israeli chamotte aided us in developing very close contact with our clients throughout Europe and in Turkey.

Later we began to develop the export of the Israeli Seawater Magnesite in Turkey, with great success. After the production of chamotte in Israel ended, we were contacted by the producer of the Ukrainian chamotte to develop their export. Again, our success was complete.

Meanwhile we increased the domestic market and the supply of raw materials and additives to the steel industry as well as abrasive industries, ceramics, glass, etc. … Today our reputation in Israel and abroad is such that it allows us to offer our customers the best products from the best producers. Producers with whom we have developed very special relationships with time, based on trust and respect.

For many years, since 1971, our company served the Israeli market in both the particular and international market in general. Our concern is to develop our relationship with our customers and to supply them with the right materials and products at the right prices.

We speciaize in raw materials for any type of industry, such as steel industry, water-jet cutting and garnet sand, casting industry and more. Here you will find information regarding our company, information on our products and also have the option to shop online in our website
